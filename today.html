The Manananggal

July 20, 2023

Once an urban myth in the Philipine countrysides, the potential sightings of the Manananggal spark fear in the hearts of Bohol locals. While we are still unsure of the authenticity of this sighting,, be sure to keep your mothers and wives safe as this creature is known for targeting pregnent women. Resembling a woman who's body has been split apart at the waist, bat-like wings growing out of its back, and a devilishly long tongue, whether this sighting is a hoax or not, DO NOT interact under any circumstances
